Contact Info

You can contact me for support in a variety of ways:
  • You can submit a bug report at the project's launchpad site: https://launchpad.net/screendump/+filebug
  • You can ask a question at the project's launchpad site: https://launchpad.net/screendump/+addquestion
  • Or you can try sending an e-mail to dev@indiecodelabs.com. Please remember to include the program version you are using and, if possible, the ID text located at the bottom of the "About..." window.
