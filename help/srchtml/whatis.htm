What is screendump?

screendump is a simple program I made when I figured most other screenshot-taking programs I've tried are either overbloated with features I don't want/need or just don't cut it. So I basically made this as simple-but-functional as I could possibly want it to be.

Just hit the Print Screen Key (or Alt+Print Screen for active window) and the screenshot is automatically saved to the hard disk. If you hit it fast enough you might get 3-12 screenshots each second*, not bad.
*Limited to your finger's quickness.
